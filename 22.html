3.Connot Labour the Favour

Grace is not license to sin! Grace is the strength to overcome sin!

Grace is often misunderstood and also, people try to earn God’s favour. Grace is not grace if it is earned. Because grace is a free gift from God. It cannot be earned by man. Do not misunderstand grace. Do not think that it encourages sin. That is completely wrong. Grace gives the strength to overcome sin. Man cannot overcome sin by his own strength or his own effort. But grace which flows from the cross gives the power to overcome sin. Sin cannot overcome you because you are under grace. His grace is sufficient for you.

For example, we insure our vehicle. We do not let our vehicle get into an accident just because we have insurance. In case of an accident, we can use the insurance. Similarly, grace is not a license to sin, it is the strength to overcome your weakness.

His grace is sufficient for your successful life.
