ABOUT OUR MINISTRY

Prophet T. Vinoth Kumar is the founder of International New Life Prophetic Ministries, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, and India. He walked with God for more than 25 years. From his young age, He obeyed God's call to serve Him.

  • We conduct pastor's training and conferences in various States of India.
  • He is the carrier of strong prophetic anointing for this end time.
  • He explores the scripture and reveals the truth with high insight of end-time revelations. God has entrusted him with the gift of prophecy. He is used by God to influence and uplift countless people's lives.
  • He have written some books ” As You Think, You Become” and "Keys of Successful Life(English & Tamil)".

He is one of the best teachers revealing the depth and breaking the ground truths of the scriptures. His teachings help many people to know the true nature of God.
